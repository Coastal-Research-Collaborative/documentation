# Managing CRC Google Group Members

Note: You can only access this through your hawaii.edu account.

Screenshots below can be clicked to view them full size.

## Adding a User

1. Navigate to the [Google Groups Console](https://groups.google.com/u/1/)

<a href={img01.src} target="_blank" rel="noopener">
  <Image src={img01} alt="Navigating to Google Group Console" width={550} />
</a>

2. Click on "CRC Core Group"
   - If "CRC Core Group" does not show up in your list, reach out to skyler8@hawaii.edu for access

<a href={img02.src} target="_blank" rel="noopener">
  <Image src={img02} alt="Navigate CRC Core Group Row" width={880} />
</a>

3. On the left sidebar, navigate to the "Members" list

<a href={img03.src} target="_blank" rel="noopener">
  <Image src={img03} alt="Members List" width={550} />
</a>

4. Click "Add members"

<a href={img04.src} target="_blank" rel="noopener">
  <Image src={img04} alt="Add members" width={880} />
</a>

5. In the "Group members" field, enter the user's hawaii.edu email
   - To also give the user a manager or owner role, enter their email in the respective field

<a href={img05.src} target="_blank" rel="noopener">
  <Image src={img05} alt="Group member fields" width={550} />
</a>

6. Click "Add members" in the pop-up modal

<a href={img06.src} target="_blank" rel="noopener">
  <Image src={img06} alt="Add members confirmation" width={550} />
</a>

## Removing a User

1. Click on the user's row (the row will be highlighted when selected)

<a href={img07.src} target="_blank" rel="noopener">
  <Image src={img07} alt="Member row selected" width={880} />
</a>

2. In the top right of the members table, click the remove icon

<a href={img07.src} target="_blank" rel="noopener">
  <Image src={img07} alt="Remove icon in members table" width={880} />
</a>

3. In the confirmation pop-up, click "OK"

<a href={img08.src} target="_blank" rel="noopener">
  <Image src={img08} alt="Confirmation modal" width={550} />
</a>
